TONY D JONES

THE CARPENTER

In 2016 Tony worked as a stagehand in the MIT theater. He built set pieces and repaired equipment and became familiar with the practice of carpentry. In 2017 he had his first major test of proficiency, building a loft bed for his friend, which he carefully designed and calculated the necessary materials before building with his friend.
Tony's first major solo project was building his own furniture after he graduated and moved to his own apartment. He designed and built a dresser and desk to his own specifications.
Later on Tony also designed and built his own bed, making all furniture in his room handmade by his own two hands.
