THE (FUTURE) PERSONAL TRAINER

In 2019 Tony started working out 6 days every week in order to be more healthy and improve his martial arts, and discovered a love of lifting on top of his love of sports. Tony perused all online resources he could find, looking for more and better ways to improve his techniques and his health.
In 2020 Tony purchased a personal trainer certification course from The American Council On Exercise, one of the best exercise regulation commissions in America. He studied the materials persistently during his off hours...and occasionally while he was working, if he had a spare moment.
Tony completed studying for the course in just 4 weeks. Although he has completed the course materials, he has not been certified yet due to delays caused by COVID-19. But once the lockdown is over, it's on!
